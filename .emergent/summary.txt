<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
The user, representing a training institute named ETI Educom, requested a full-fledged lead management system.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **Lead Management:**
  - Capture leads with details: name, numbers, address, email, program, fee quoted, payment plan, source.
  - Manage lead statuses: New, Contacted, Demo Booked, Follow-up, Converted, Lost.
  - A dedicated page for managing leads.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):**
  - **Admin:** Superuser with full access. Can create branches, programs, and other users. Can view all data across all branches.
  - **Counsellor:** Can add/manage leads and follow-ups for their assigned branch only.
  - **Front Desk Executive (FDE):** Can manage expenses for their branch. Can view converted leads in their branch and enroll them.
- **User and Branch Management:**
  - Admin can create/edit/delete branches with comprehensive details (name, location, owner info, contact).
  - Admin can create/edit/delete users with detailed personal info (DOB, contact, address, photo). Users are assigned to a specific role and branch.
- **Program Management:**
  - Admin can create/edit/delete programs with details like duration, fee, and max allowed discount.
- **Follow-ups:**
  - Counsellors can schedule follow-ups with a specific date and time.
  - A Pending Follow-ups page should display follow-ups due on the current day.
- **Enrollment and Payments (FDE Role):**
  - FDE enrolls converted leads by filling out a multi-step form (personal, academic, program, payment details).
  - The system should handle both one-time payments and installments.
  - FDE can configure payment installments (number, amount, due dates).
  - The system must record all payments and be able to generate receipts.
- **Expense Management (FDE Role):**
  - Admin defines expense categories.
  - FDE records branch-specific expenses against these categories.
- **Dashboard & Analytics:**
  - Admin dashboard to show branch-wise performance reports (total leads, conversions, status breakdown).
- **Integrations:**
  - **MSG91 WhatsApp API:** Automate messages on lead creation and status updates.
- **UI/UX:**
  - A professional and corporate look and feel.

**User's preferred language**: English
